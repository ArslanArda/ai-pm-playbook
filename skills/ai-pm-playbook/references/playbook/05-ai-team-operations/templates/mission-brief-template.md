# Mission Brief Template

Use this template to kick off a 6-week AI mission.

## Mission Summary

**Mission name:** [e.g., Conversational Search Beta Readiness]  
**Mission owner:** [e.g., AI PM]  
**Cycle dates:** [e.g., May 6 - June 14]  
**Core question:** [e.g., Can we launch conversational search to 5% of mobile users with acceptable quality and latency?]

## Why This Mission Matters

- User problem: [e.g., users struggle to express complex housing intent with rigid filters]
- Business reason: [e.g., search abandonment is highest for multi-constraint sessions]
- Why now: [e.g., Q2 roadmap priority and existing prototype maturity]

## Mission Success Criteria

- Product outcome: [e.g., beta-ready experience for defined cohort]
- Quality threshold: [e.g., relevance >= 4.2/5, hallucination rate <= 2%]
- Operational threshold: [e.g., P95 latency <= 5s]
- Decision outcome: [e.g., ship beta / narrow scope / hold launch]

## In Scope

- [e.g., intent extraction for Turkish mobile search]
- [e.g., clarification logic for missing rent-vs-buy signal]
- [e.g., beta observability and fallback behavior]

## Out Of Scope

- [e.g., multilingual support beyond Turkish]
- [e.g., persistent memory across sessions]
- [e.g., neighborhood recommendation engine]

## Team And Roles

- PM: [name]
- Engineering lead: [name]
- Design: [name]
- Data / analytics: [name]
- Ops / support: [name if relevant]

## Key Risks

- [e.g., silent constraint drop in ambiguous queries]
- [e.g., clarification loops increase abandonment]
- [e.g., latency budget missed during peak load]

## Decision Rights

- Scope decisions: [e.g., PM drives, product lead approves]
- Model decisions: [e.g., engineering lead recommends, PM approves]
- Launch gate: [e.g., VP Product approves after PM recommendation]

## Weekly Review Questions

- What did we learn this week?
- What quality signal changed?
- What is the main blocker?
- What should be cut rather than expanded?

## End-Of-Cycle Decision

- [ ] Ship to defined cohort
- [ ] Extend mission with narrowed scope
- [ ] Pause or stop
- [ ] Roll into larger platform initiative
