# Reading List

This is a practical list for PMs, not an attempt to catalog all AI literature.

## Product And Strategy

- Product thinking on AI-native workflows, especially around uncertainty, iteration, and user trust
- Case studies from companies shipping AI search, support automation, and content-generation products
- Leadership writing on cost-latency-quality tradeoffs and AI rollout discipline

## Evaluation And Reliability

- Material on evaluation design for LLM systems
- Writing on grader reliability, rubric creation, and human-vs-model agreement
- Resources on failure analysis, red-teaming, and regression testing for AI products

## UX And Human Factors

- Research on trust, transparency, and user response to automation
- Work on conversational UX, latency perception, and correction flows
- Design writing on human-in-the-loop systems and confidence communication

## Organizational Adoption

- Change-management and adoption work focused on practical task integration
- Writing on cross-functional operating models for AI teams
- Internal enablement case studies where AI tools succeeded or failed based on rollout design

## How To Use This List

Do not read everything. Pick the category that matches your current bottleneck:

- unclear product strategy: start with product and strategy
- weak launch confidence: start with evaluation and reliability
- user trust problems: start with UX and human factors
- slow internal uptake: start with organizational adoption
